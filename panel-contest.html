Kivy App Contest 2014

How much Kivy is inside you ?

Kivy is launching its second application development contest! This is a great chance for new and experienced users to show off their skills and compete for prizes. Entries will be judged on a range of criteria accessible to both new and experienced programmers, so don't be afraid to dive in!

Guidelines

These are general guidelines, exact rules and terms will be posted before the contest starts. The goal is to show the world some of the great things that can be built quickly with Kivy while having fun, and to give more people an opportunity to try out Kivy's cool features. The general guidelines are:
  • Keep it family friendly.
  • Make it fun and attractive, show how your Kivy app can stand out from the crowd.
  • Entries can be from individuals or from teams.
  • No prior code - build your full app during the competition month. You are allowed to re-use existing known open source libraries in conjunction with Kivy.
  • Don't forget the theme (to be revealed)!
